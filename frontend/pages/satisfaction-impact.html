Delay Impact on Satisfaction
How flight delays degrade passenger satisfaction rate
Flights with severe delays (>120 min) see satisfaction drop to just 21.8% — a 40+ percentage point collapse from on-time performance. The Delay Recovery Voucher recommendation directly targets this high-risk group.
Class & Travel Type Impact
Structural satisfaction differences by passenger category
Economy class personal travelers represent the largest dissatisfied segment — 43% of all passengers, yet only 39% satisfaction rate. This is the highest-opportunity group for recommendation-driven uplift.
